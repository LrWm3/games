About the hub

A quiet home for playful prototypes, demos, and guides.

LRWM3 Games keeps everything lightweight and fast. It is designed so visitors can quickly find a game, get in, and start playing. Every game and guide lives on a simple static page for instant loads on Cloudflare Pages.

Browse games Read a guide

Principles

What matters here

  • Fast navigation and zero clutter so players stay focused.
  • Clear labels for playable builds vs demos.
  • Mobile-first layouts that look crisp on any device.
  • Static hosting for quick loads and easy updates.

What you can do

Use this space as your launchpad

Play

Jump into full builds from the playable shelf and keep your session moving.

Explore

Peek at experimental demos to see what is in the lab right now.

Learn

Grab short guides for how-to tasks and rapid inspiration.

Next steps

Want to add new games or guides?

Drop a new HTML file into the right folder and add it to the list. The site is designed to stay simple, so adding fresh content is as easy as updating one array.

Games live in /games/playable and /games/demos.

Guides live in /guides.

Update the arrays in index.html and games/index.html.
